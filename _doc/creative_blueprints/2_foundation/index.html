Why CaterFlow

The Problem

Catering owners juggling multiple events lean on spreadsheets, sticky notes, and group chats. The result: missed details, double-booked staff, mismatched menus — and quality that suffers precisely when it matters most.

The Solution

CaterFlow is a single, purpose-built workspace that connects every event order, menu, and staff assignment — giving catering owners complete visibility and control across all their events at once.
